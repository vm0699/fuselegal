Fuse – Child Safety Standards

These standards publish Fuse’s policies and processes to prevent, detect, and respond to child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). They apply to all users and content on Fuse.

Scope & age policy

  • 18+ only: Fuse is a dating & social app strictly for adults. Minors (under 18) are not allowed to create accounts or use the service.
  • Zero tolerance: We prohibit any content or behavior that harms, sexualizes, exploits, grooms, or endangers minors.
  • Definitions: “Minor” means any person under 18 years old. “CSAE” includes child sexual abuse material (CSAM), grooming, sexual solicitation of a minor, sextortion, trafficking, and facilitation of such acts.

Prohibited content & behavior

  • Any depiction, solicitation, sharing, or request involving sexualized content of a minor (real or purported), including AI‑generated/“virtual” minors and cartoons.
  • Grooming, sexual propositions to someone believed to be a minor, attempts to obtain sexual images, sextortion, or arranging meetings with minors.
  • Endangerment or exploitation of children, including doxxing, trafficking, facilitating commercial sexual exploitation, or instructions to evade law‑enforcement.
  • Impersonating a minor or misrepresenting age; uploading images that appear to be minors; or content that sexualizes young‑looking persons.

Prevention & moderation

  • Access controls: New users must be 18+. Accounts suspected to belong to minors are restricted and reviewed.
  • User tools: Every profile created can access the developers phone numbers and report any misbehaviour they feel which needs to be addressed.
  • Review: Reported content is triaged; clear CSAE is removed and accounts are disabled immediately. We preserve evidence as required by law.
  • Repeat offenders: Permanently banned; device/account signals may be used to prevent return.

How to report concerns

Report in‑app using by conveying it to the developers contacting them through their given phone numbers or email us directly:

  • Safety & Abuse: datingfuse@gmail.com
  • Support: datingfuse@gmail.com

Include any relevant usernames, screenshots, and links. We will acknowledge urgent CSAE reports as quickly as possible, typically within 1 hour, and aim to take action within 24 hours.

Cooperation with authorities

  • We promptly escalate confirmed CSAE to appropriate law‑enforcement and relevant hotlines where applicable.
  • We preserve and disclose data in response to valid legal requests. We also take steps to prevent re‑uploads.

User responsibilities

  • Do not upload, request, or distribute content involving minors or young‑looking persons.
  • Do not attempt to contact or solicit minors. If you suspect a user is under 18, stop interaction and report immediately.

Data handling for safety reports

  • Information submitted in a report is used solely for trust & safety review and legal compliance.
  • We retain relevant data for as long as necessary to meet legal obligations and prevent abuse, consistent with our Privacy Policy.

Resources (external help)

If you believe someone is in immediate danger, contact local emergency services first.

  • IN: National Cyber Crime Reporting Portal
  • US: NCMEC CyberTipline
  • UK: CEOP Safety Centre

Compliance statement

Fuse complies with applicable child protection and online safety laws and will continue to update these standards to reflect regulatory guidance and best practices.
