Fuse – Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 11/8/2025 • Last Updated: 11/8/2025

Fuse (“we”, “our”, “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share information when you use our mobile application and related services.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide: Name, phone number, date of birth, gender, photos, interests, and other profile fields you choose to share; messages/compliments; feedback/support requests.

Information collected automatically: Device details, usage analytics, coarse/precise location if enabled, and camera/microphone access for video calls and profile photos (we do not record video/audio unless clearly stated).

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide and improve matchmaking, messaging, and video call features
  • Personalize experiences based on your preferences
  • Safety, security, fraud prevention, and legal compliance

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited data with:

  • Other users – profile information you choose to share
  • Service providers – e.g., hosting (AWS/S3), real‑time communications (Twilio/Agora), analytics—only to operate the app
  • Legal authorities – if required by law or to protect rights and safety

4. Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure cloud servers with encryption and access controls. No method is 100% secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect it.

5. Your Choices & Rights

  • Access/update your profile in the app
  • Delete your account and associated data via in‑app delete or email us at datingfuse@gmail.com
  • Withdraw permissions (camera, mic, location) in device settings

6. Children’s Privacy

Fuse is for users 18+ only. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

7. Third‑Party Links

Links to third‑party sites/services are not controlled by us. Review their privacy practices.

8. Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here with a revised effective date.
